Problem Statement

The State Crime Records Bureau (SCRB) manages a large and continuously expanding repository of crime-related data from 1100+ police stations across Karnataka. Current systems rely on static dashboards and manual queries, limiting deep analysis and real-time insights.

🎯

The Challenge

Build an intelligent conversational AI platform enabling investigators to query crime data using natural language and uncover patterns, relationships, and predictive insights.

Crime Pattern Discovery Criminal Network Analysis Socio-demographic Insights Behavioral Profiling Proactive Crime Prevention
🔑

Core Capabilities

Everything You Need to Fight Crime Intelligently

Built for Karnataka Police officers — from constables to senior IPS officers

Natural Language Queries

Ask questions in plain English, Hindi, or Kannada. No SQL or complex filters needed. The AI understands police terminology and local context.

Crime Hotspot Detection

Real-time geospatial analysis identifies emerging hotspots across districts, taluks, and police station jurisdictions with predictive alerts.

Criminal Network Analysis

Visualise connections between suspects, associates, and criminal organisations. Uncover hidden links across multiple cases and districts.

Predictive Intelligence

ML-powered predictions on crime occurrence, recidivism likelihood, and seasonal trends to proactively deploy resources.

Role-Based Access Control

Strict access tiers from constable to DGP. Officers see only data within their jurisdiction. Full audit trail for every query.

Smart Reports & Export

Auto-generate formatted investigation reports, statistical summaries, and court-ready documents with one click. PDF & Excel export.

Voice-Enabled Queries

Speak your queries in Kannada, Hindi, or English. Ideal for field officers using mobile devices during active operations.

Real-Time SCRB Sync

Live connection to SCRB databases. Data is updated as FIRs are registered across all 1100+ police stations in Karnataka.

Explainable AI (XAI)

Every AI conclusion comes with a transparent reasoning chain. Officers can understand why a pattern was flagged, ensuring accountability.

Process

How O.R.C.A Works

A seamless, secure pipeline from query to actionable intelligence

01

Officer Submits Query

Login with your O.R.C.A credentials. Type or speak your question in any of the 3 supported languages. The system verifies your role and jurisdiction automatically.

02

AI Processes & Analyses

The NLP engine translates natural language to structured queries. Machine learning models run pattern detection, network analysis, and predictive algorithms on SCRB data.

03

Insights Delivered

Results are displayed as interactive charts, maps, network graphs, and plain-language summaries. Export as PDF, share with team, or escalate for action.
